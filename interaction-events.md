# Interaction Events Content

## Coach Gonzalez

### Tier 1 — "The Coach Has Notes" (rel ≥ 0)
**Dialogue:**
- Coach: "Sit down. I have seventeen things to tell you."
- Player: "...all right."
- Coach: "I'll start with the most important one. Your footwork in the third set last week—"
- [narration]: "He opens the clipboard. There are so many diagrams."

**Result:** "You spend the next hour nodding along while Coach Gonzalez covers the clipboard in increasingly ambitious arrows. Some of it sinks in. Most of it is just arrows."

**Effects:** serve+1, forehand+1, mood+5, energy-20, coach_gonzalez relationship+3

---

### Tier 2 — "Film Room Frenzy" (rel ≥ 25, requires tier 1)
**Dialogue:**
- Coach: "Today we watch tape. Three hours. Maybe four."
- Player: "Four hours?"
- Coach: "Quiet. It's starting."
- [narration]: "He dims the lights. He has popcorn. You do not get any of the popcorn."

**Result:** "Coach pauses the footage approximately forty times to yell at the screen. By the end you're not sure if you learned tennis or just absorbed his opinions about tennis. Either way, something clicked."

**Effects:** serve+2, anticipation+1, mood+8, energy-20, coach_gonzalez relationship+4

---

### Tier 3 — "The Grand Tactical Breakdown" (rel ≥ 50, requires tier 2)
**Dialogue:**
- Coach: "Today, we solve your game. Completely."
- [narration]: "He has rented a second whiteboard."
- Player: "Did you... rent a whiteboard?"
- Coach: "Focus."

**Result:** "The whiteboard runs out of space. Coach begins writing on a napkin. You have to make a call."

**Choice A — Serve Focus:** "You dedicate the session to serve mechanics. Coach draws so many ball trajectories the napkin tears."
- Effects: serve+3, placement+1, mood+10, energy-20, relationship+4

**Choice B — Return Focus:** "You focus on reading the opponent's serve. Coach makes you close your eyes and 'feel the ball coming.'"
- Effects: return+3, anticipation+1, mood+10, energy-20, relationship+4

---

### Tier 4 — "The Secret Playbook" (rel ≥ 75, requires tier 3)
**Dialogue:**
- [narration]: "Coach locks the door. Actual lock. Key and everything."
- Coach: "What I'm about to show you does not leave this room."
- Player: "...okay."
- Coach: "I mean it. Not even the other coaches."

**Result:** "He opens a second, smaller clipboard from inside the first clipboard. You didn't know that was possible. This one has the real diagrams."

**Choice A — Power Game:** "You lean into the power. More pace, more pressure, more everything."
- Effects: serve+2, forehand+2, stamina+1, mood+15, energy-20, relationship+5, **Ability: Grand Strategist**

**Choice B — Finesse Game:** "You study the subtle stuff. Angles, spins, the tiny adjustments nobody else thinks about."
- Effects: slice+2, dropShot+2, focus+1, mood+15, energy-20, relationship+5, **Ability: Grand Strategist**

### Tier 3 — "Hit the Wall Together" (rel ≥ 50, requires tier 2)
**Dialogue:**

- Player: "Is this a challenge?"

**Result:** "Nobody officially wins. That's not the point. You push each other harder than you would alone, which is somehow worse and better at the same time."

**Choice A — Go Aggressive:** "You spend the session hammering winners. Jordan responds by hitting harder. Your arm is going to hate you tomorrow."
- Effects: offensive+2, serve+1, energy-15, relationship+4

**Choice B — Stay Defensive:** "You focus on getting everything back. Jordan tests your range. You test their patience."
