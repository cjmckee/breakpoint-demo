## Notes on Event Development

When developing these ideas into full events, consider:

- **Character consistency**: Ensure recurring characters stay true to their established personalities
- **Meaningful choices**: Options should reflect different values/approaches, not just good/bad
- **Stat relevance**: Prerequisites and rewards should align with event theme
- **Emotional range**: Balance celebration, conflict, growth, and setback events
- **Interconnection**: Events can reference or build on previous events
- **Player agency**: Even linear events should make player feel their choices matter
- **Realism**: Ground fantastical elements in authentic tennis/athlete experiences
- **Pacing**: Mix heavy emotional events with lighter character moments
